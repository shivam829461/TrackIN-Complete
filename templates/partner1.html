OUR PARTNERS

  • Amazon

    Amazon is a popular e-commerce platform and one of the world's largest online marketplaces. Over the years, Amazon has expanded its offerings to include a wide range of products such as electronics, clothing, home goods, books, music, movies, and much more.

  • Flipkart

    Flipkart is one of the largest and most well-known e-commerce platforms in India. Founded in 2007, Flipkart initially focused on selling books but has now expanded to offer a wide range of products across categories such as electronics, fashion, home appliances, beauty, and more.

  • Myntra

    Myntra is a leading online fashion and lifestyle retailer in India. Launched in 2007, Myntra specializes in offering a vast selection of clothing, footwear, accessories, and beauty products for men, women, and children.

  • Snapdeal

    Snapdeal is another prominent e-commerce platform in India that offers a diverse range of products across categories like electronics, fashion, home goods, appliances, and more. Founded in 2010, Snapdeal connects buyers and sellers from across the country, providing a vast marketplace with millions of products.

  • Bluedart

    Blue Dart is a leading logistics and courier company in India. Established in 1983, it offers a comprehensive range of delivery services, including domestic and international express deliveries, freight transportation, and supply chain solutions. Blue Dart is known for its extensive reach, efficient delivery network, and strong customer service.

  • Delhivery

    Delhivery is a prominent logistics and supply chain services provider in India. Founded in 2011, it has rapidly grown to become one of the largest e-commerce fulfillment networks in the country.
